Technology


Adding a racing touch without exceeding legal noise output limits: our approved silencers are made to replace those provided with your Panigale V4 set, increasing the performance.

Materials


Inspired by the racing world, the silencers are made in an exclusive titanium alloy with precious carbon endcaps. A honeycomb grille protects the exhaust end.

Performance


Lighter and more performing. The couple of approved silencers means that the bike is 1 kg lighter and the Panigale V4 weight-ratio is improved. Moreover, the exhaust system allows a stronger delivery and a grittier sound.

Design


Behind the style of our approved silencers lie the same hand that designed the lines of the Panigale V4: the Ducati designer team worked to make this exhaust system fully integrated into the look of the bike, for a result that will certainly have an impact.
